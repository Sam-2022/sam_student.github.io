Board Games!

I myself cannot define board games. Board games might be RPG's or a digital hybrids. There are PNP (print & play) games that do not require any form of transactions. But again, please observe quarantine rules.

Role-playing Forums

RPG forums are online communities readily available to ease irritability. Brikwars, Wesworld, Shipbucket and some fanfiction websites are scattered across the World Wide Web.
